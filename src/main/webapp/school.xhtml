MNR High School was established in 2002 and it is managed by the School Management. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 10 . The school is co-educational and English is the medium of instructions in this school. The school academic session starts in June. This school follows the State board. The School is located in Chandanagar block of Ranga Reddy,Hyderabad in the state of TELANGANA and is approachable by all weather road

The academic system followed at MNR Day and Residential schools is innovative and experimental, which in all humility, makes the learning process an enlightening one and is relevant to the challenges of the future. Teaching at MNR schools is student-centered, project based, and incorporates the multiple intelligence concepts.

Our aim is to develop motivated, confident and articulate students with all the skills necessary for independent study. The students are encouraged to develop talents and interests in personal areas of academic interest, or in sports, theatre, music and dance. Every class room is equipped with two monitors and a computer to provide multi-media presentations through more than 36,000 3D and 2D modules available exclusively for our students and to make teaching and learning more effective.
